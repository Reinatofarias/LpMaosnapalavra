## Part 2: Prompts para Geração das Imagens (AI Prompts)

Abaixo estão os prompts estruturados e testados para criação de assets em alta definição. 

> [!TIP]
> Para o **Midjourney v6**, utilize os parâmetros `--style raw` para fotos e adicione a paleta de cores para manter a consistência visual entre as gerações.

### 1. Hero Principal - Criança e Livro
* **Prompt (Midjourney):**
  > A 5-year-old boy and a 6-year-old girl sitting together at a wooden table in a sunlit cozy living room, happily coloring biblical drawings on printed paper sheets with colored pencils. In the center of the table, a beautiful open children's book showing coloring pages of Noah's Ark. Warm cozy atmosphere, soft natural light, editorial premium style, soft pastel color palette (warm terracotta, soft olive green, cream background), watercolor and colored pencil texture, charming and emotional, highly detailed --ar 4:3 --v 6.0 --style raw
* **Elementos Proibidos:** Telas, smartphones, tablets, TVs, cores neon, traço 3D estilo Pixar brilhante, visual cartoon de plástico.

### 2. Mockup Principal do Livro Mãos na Palavra™
* **Prompt (Midjourney):**
  > An open children's coloring book lying on a clean light wooden table. The pages are made of thick textured paper, showing a beautiful black and white biblical line art drawing of Jesus calming the storm for kids to color. Next to the book are a few wooden colored pencils. Soft warm sunlight casting gentle shadows. Clean beige background, premium editorial design, realistic mockup, high-end children's book photography --ar 4:3 --v 6.0 --style raw --c 5
* **Elementos Proibidos:** Livros fechados, espirais de metal feios, fundos escuros ou pretos.

### 3. Mascote: Mila (A Menina Curiosa)
* **Prompt (Midjourney):**
  > A cute 6-year-old Brazilian girl character, curly brown hair tied in two pigtails, wearing denim overalls over a terracotta t-shirt, holding a giant yellow crayon, smiling joyfully. Children's book illustration style, soft watercolor texture, hand-drawn pencil outline, warm cozy pastel colors, isolated on a solid white background for easy cutout --ar 1:1 --v 6.0 --style raw
* **Elementos Proibidos:** Fundo complexo, estilo anime, arte vetorial flat moderna, traço de logotipo corporativo.

### 4. Mascote: Ben (O Menino Focado)
* **Prompt (Midjourney):**
  > A cute 5-year-old boy character, straight light brown hair with bangs, wearing a green and beige striped t-shirt and brown shorts, sitting on the floor, deeply focused on coloring a sheet of paper with a blue pencil. Children's book illustration style, soft watercolor wash, hand-drawn texture, warm pastel colors, isolated on a solid white background --ar 1:1 --v 6.0 --style raw
* **Elementos Proibidos:** Roupas modernas com estampas de marcas, tablets nas mãos, olhos esbugalhados de desenho animado comercial.

### 5. Mascote: Algodão (A Ovelhinha Feliz - Garantia)
* **Prompt (Midjourney):**
  > A super cute, fluffy round sheep character, looking like a soft white cloud, with short light brown legs and floppy ears, closed eyes with a peaceful and happy expression. Children's book illustration style, soft watercolor texture, warm pastel colors, holding a small olive branch in its mouth, isolated on a solid white background --ar 1:1 --v 6.0 --style raw
* **Elementos Proibidos:** Visual de desenho animado 3D hiper-realista, olhos abertos e assustados, traço vetorial de logo tecnológico.

### 6. Mascote: Algodão (A Ovelhinha Triste/Pensativa com Celular - Seção Dor)
* **Prompt (Midjourney):**
  > A cute fluffy round sheep character, looking sad and thoughtful, sitting next to a small gray tablet with a low battery icon on the screen. Soft watercolor illustration style, textured hand-drawn look, muted pastel colors, isolated on a solid white background --ar 1:1 --v 6.0 --style raw
* **Elementos Proibidos:** Rostos humanos, expressões raivosas, traço digital limpo demais.

### 7. Bônus 1: Plataforma de Histórias em Áudio
* **Prompt (Midjourney):**

Next to the phone are a pair of simple wooden kid's headphones. Warm natural lighting, soft focus, cozy home atmosphere, premium editorial feel, pastel color palette --ar 4:3 --v 6.0 --style raw
* **Elementos Proibidos:** Fones de ouvido de gamer pretos piscando LED, mãos segurando o celular, telas de aplicativos complexas e cheias de texto.

### 8. Bônus 2: Kit de Dobraduras Cristãs
* **Prompt (Midjourney):**

Soft children's book illustration style, warm sunbeams, cozy and creative setting, pastel tones, highly tactile --ar 4:3 --v 6.0 --style raw
* **Elementos Proibidos:** Dobraduras de origami japonesas complexas, cola escorrendo, tesouras de metal perigosas.

### 9. Bônus 3: Guia Cantinho Cristão Sem Telas
* **Prompt (Midjourney):**
  > A cozy children's play corner in a house. A small wooden table with two chairs, a soft cream rug, colorful cushions on the floor, and children's drawings of Bible stories pinned gently to a warm beige wall. Soft natural light coming from a nearby window. Calm, peaceful, creative, tidy, no electronics, editorial home decor style, pastel color palette --ar 4:3 --v 6.0 --style raw

### 10. Ilustração Conceitual: A Transição de Telas para Atividades (Big Idea)
* **Prompt (Midjourney):**
  > A conceptual children's book illustration. On the left side, a child's hand is dropping a gray, cold-toned smartphone. On the right side, the hand is receiving a warm, brightly colored pencil that draws a glowing star and a path of green leaves. Creative transition, symbolic of leaving screens and embracing hands-on creativity and faith. Soft watercolor and colored pencil textures, pastel tones, emotional --ar 4:3 --v 6.0
* **Elementos Proibidos:** Rostos de crianças irritadas, fracionamento da imagem com linhas duras de divisão.
